# Legal and safety boundaries

## Authorization
- Only analyze targets you own or have explicit permission to test.
- Stay within the scope of the authorization agreement.

## Prohibited actions
- No DRM/circumvention or bypass of protections.
- No access to private user data or credentials.
- No distribution of proprietary code or decrypted artifacts.

## Evidence handling
- Record only necessary artifacts.
- Redact sensitive data before sharing reports.
- Keep findings evidence-based and verifiable.
